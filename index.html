TexasGaushala

Loving Cows

Preserving Vedic System

Cows are integral part of the farm sustainability and cow urine- and dung-based bio-fertilizers are used to grow herbal/medicinal plants to produce health supplements and spices. TexasGaushala is a protective shelter for cows. It is a modern infrastructure for cultivation of indigenous cows. Thriving in a natural healthy environment, the cows at TexasGaushala feed on checmical-free fodder and a nutritional diet.

At TexasGaushala, we offer various services like Gau Pooja, Cow feeding, etc. In addition, we offer skill and development training programs for visitors, farmers and students on regenerative agricultural concepts.
